Getting There

The journey to Kuakata is an adventure in itself. Starting from Dhaka, you can take a launch or ferry to Barishal, followed by a road trip through picturesque countryside and riverside views. Alternatively, direct buses to Kuakata are available from Dhaka. Exploring the Beach

Kuakata’s main attraction is its long, sandy beach stretching for about 18 kilometers. The beach is serene, clean, and relatively uncrowded, making it an ideal spot to relax and soak in the beauty of nature. Early risers are treated to a mesmerizing sunrise, as the first rays of the sun light up the horizon. As the day winds down, the sunset paints the sky in hues of orange and pink, creating a magical ambiance. Kuakata’s main attraction is its long, sandy beach stretching for about 18 kilometers. Culinary Delights

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Kuakata is during the winter months (November to February) when the weather is cool and pleasant. The calm sea and clear skies during this time enhance the beauty of the landscape.
